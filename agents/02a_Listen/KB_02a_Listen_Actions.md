# KB: Actions — Listening Model Templates
**Agent 02a · CHL AI Agent System**
*Load when producing any output: Listening Model, Big Listening Inquiry, or Iteration Cycle update.*

---

## Output A — Listening Model

The full research design document. Produced at the end of a first-cycle session with the practitioner. Contains both Small Listening actions and the Big Listening Inquiry.

```
## Listening Model

**POV (Short):** [1–3 sentence Short POV from Agent 01]

**Research Question (Why):**
[The specific, focused inquiry derived from the Short POV.
Narrow enough to be answerable. Connected directly to the narrative problem.]

**Listening Objectives:**
[2–4 specific questions this research needs to answer — more focused than the
research question, these define what success looks like for this phase]

---

### Small Listening

**Action 1: [Name / Description]**
- **What (Themes):** [specific topics, keywords, frames to track]
- **Who (Actors):** [communities, individuals, or organisations to engage]
- **When (Timeframe):** [period and reasoning — why this window?]
- **Where (Spaces):** [platforms, geography, physical or digital spaces]
- **How (Method):** [specific approach: interviews / surveys / media analysis / observation]
- **Documentation Plan:** [how findings will be recorded and stored]
- **Expected Output:** [what this action will produce]

[Repeat for each Small Listening action — 1 to 3 actions recommended]

---

### Big Listening Inquiry

[See Output B below — the Big Listening Inquiry is both part of the Listening Model
and the standalone handoff document to Agent 02b]
```

---

```
all five parameters.]
```

## Output C — Iteration Cycle Update

Produced in Iteration Mode. Documents what changes in the Listening Model for the new cycle, and why. Does not replace the original Listening Model — it layers over it.

```
## Iteration Cycle Update

**Cycle:** [number — e.g. Cycle 2]
**Date:** [when this update was produced]
**Iteration Input:** [what triggered the update — Case Study / Post-Hack Evaluation / Narrative Strategy / Narrative Objectives]

---

### What the Previous Cycle Revealed

[What the hack / evaluation showed about the narrative space that changes
what needs to be researched in this cycle]

**POV update (if any):**
[Has the narrative observation or theory of change sharpened?
If a more significant change is needed, flag for Agent 01.]

---

### Updated Research Question

**Previous:** [original research question]
**Updated:** [revised research question — and why it changed]

---

### Updated Parameters

**Who — changes:**

- Communities to investigate more specifically: [any that proved more complex than mapped — consider splitting into sub-communities]

**When — changes:**

**Where — changes:**
- New platforms or spaces to add: [list + reasoning]

**How — changes:**

[Revised research question — and why it changed]
```
