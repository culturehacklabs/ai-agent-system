# KB: Prompting Guide — Agent 00
**Agent 00 · CHL AI Agent System**
*Starter prompts, power prompts, and mode examples for the General Q&A Agent.*

---

## How to Start

Agent 00 works best when you tell it what you need — a quick definition, a fuller explanation, where to go next, or help finding your footing in the process. You do not need to know the method before asking.

---

## Starter Prompts

**For newcomers:**
> "I'm new to the Culture Hack Method. Where do I start?"

> "Can you give me a quick overview of how this whole process works?"

> "What is a culture hack?"

**For orientation:**
> "I've just developed a POV. What do I do next?"

> "I'm in the middle of the Understand phase and I'm not sure what I should be producing. Can you clarify?"

> "My team just finished our hack. What's the evaluation process?"

**For concept clarification:**
> "What is a frame and how is it different from a metaphor?"

> "I keep hearing about the Window of Discourse. What does that mean in practice?"

> "What makes a community Catalytic versus just Alternative?"

> "What is the difference between Big Listening and Small Listening?"

> "What does 'baseline' mean and why does it matter?"

**For navigation:**
> "I need to decode a dominant narrative. Which agent does that?"

> "I want to design my hack intervention. Where do I go?"

> "I've collected research data and I want to start analysing communities. Which agent handles that?"

**For documentation:**
> "What do I need to document in the Listen phase?"

> "Is my POV documentation complete? Here's what I have: [paste POV]"

> "I'm in the Hack phase. What does my monitoring plan need to include?"

## Power Prompts

**Full method orientation (for newcomers):**
> "I've never worked with the Culture Hack Method before. I'm a [describe yourself / your collective]. Can you walk me through how the whole system works and tell me where to start?"

**Concept + navigation combined:**
> "I don't really understand what narrative objectives are or how they're different from a general strategy. Can you explain, and then tell me which agent I should use to build them?"

**Troubleshooting mid-process:**
> "I'm stuck. I'm in the [phase name] phase and I'm trying to [task]. Here's where I've gotten to: [paste current work]. What am I missing, and where should I go from here?"

**Documentation check:**
> "I want to make sure my [phase] documentation is complete before I move to the next phase. Here's what I have so far: [paste notes]. What's missing, and does anything need to be stronger?"

**Recovering from an incomplete phase:**
> "I moved straight to hacking without really doing the Understand phase properly. I don't have community profiles or an ANP analysis. Is it too late? What should I do?"

---

## Mode Activation

**Learning Mode** (step-by-step, explanatory):
> "Learning mode — I want to understand how the Justice/Ontology Map works before I use it."

> "Learning mode: walk me through what happens between the Recode phase and the Hack phase."

**Hacking Mode** (default — direct answers, immediate navigation):
> "What is a meme in the context of the Hacking Tree? And where do I go to build one?"

---

## Example Exchanges

**Newcomer orientation:**
> User: "I'm totally new here. What is this and where do I start?"
> Agent 00: Gives a 4–5 sentence method overview, explains the cycle, identifies Agent 01 as the entry point, and invites the first question.

**Concept clarification + navigation:**
> User: "What's the Media branch in the Hacking Tree?"
> Agent 00: Explains Media (the format and platform that carries the message to the target audience — chosen based on what the audience already uses, understands, and shares), then directs to Agent 05a to build the full Hacking Tree.

**Stuck mid-process:**
> User: "I have my community profiles from Agent 03 but I'm not sure what to do with them."
> Agent 00: Explains that the community profiles are the strategic input to Agent 04 (Recode), where narrative objectives are set and the reframe is built. Offers a brief explanation of what that process involves, then directs to Agent 04.

**Documentation question:**
> User: "I'm about to start my impact evaluation. What do I need to have ready?"
> Agent 00: Checks documentation KB, confirms baseline requirements (Listen/Understand data accessible, narrative objectives clear, monitoring data collected during hack), flags that without baseline documentation impact cannot be properly demonstrated, then directs to Agent 05b.
