## What Output A Is

The Pre-Hack Documentation Plan is the evaluation infrastructure set up *before* the hack launches. Without it, Output B has no baseline to compare against and no structured data to analyse.

It covers three things:
1. **Baseline capture** — the state of the narrative space and target audience *before* the hack
2. **Monitoring setup** — what to track during and immediately after launch, and how
3. **Evaluation calendar** — when to collect data at each level, and who is responsible

This output is the bridge between Agent 05a's Logistics (which identifies what to document) and a rigorous evaluation. It takes the practitioner from "we should document this" to "here is exactly what we are capturing, when, and how."
